Take Pro-Choice Ideas Seriously

Pro-choice people are our peers. Ultimately, we think the best arguments and evidence support the pro-life position, but you should take pro-choice arguments seriously. People defending abortion deserve genuine responses to the things they say. If we all try to understand each other, we’ll be in a better position to reach a consensus about abortion.
