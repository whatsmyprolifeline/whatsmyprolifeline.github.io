Never Accept Rape

Abortion, like rape, is an act of violence. With love and compassion, we should assist rape victims in nonviolent ways. For example, we should ensure that rape victims have access to counseling, and when a pregnant rape victim chooses to place her child for adoption, we should help with the process.
