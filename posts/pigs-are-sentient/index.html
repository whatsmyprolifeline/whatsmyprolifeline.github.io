Pigs Are Sentient

People disagree about what counts as sentience, but it seems like if a newborn infant is sentient, then a pig should be too. This is important, because most people agree that we should grant more rights to an infant than to a pig. (Animal abuse is not okay, but for example, it’s a good idea to test medical products on animals before humans.)
