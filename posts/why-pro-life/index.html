Why Are You Pro-Life?

Be prepared to give a more comprehensive defense of the pro-life position, but this is a good soundbite. Given more time, explain that you’re pro-life because you think you have good arguments for the following three points. First, a fetus is an individual living human, biologically speaking. Second, every member of the human species is a person (a being with the same value, serious moral status, and basic rights that adults have). Third, the basic rights of persons include the right to not be killed through abortion.
