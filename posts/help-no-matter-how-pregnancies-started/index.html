Help No Matter How Pregnancies Started

Many pro-choice people have a hard time believing that pro-life people genuinely care about protecting human life. Some pro-choice people think that secretly, pro-life people want to punish women for having immoral sex by causing them to suffer through pregnancy. In reality, we want to help and support all pregnant women through nonviolent means. We’re against abortion even when a wife and husband intentionally conceive a child and then regret it.
